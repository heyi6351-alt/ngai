“Dim Sum Card”
Calendar 2025

Category: Graphic / Cultural
Year: 2025
Service: Print & Concept

This 2025 calendar is designed after the classic dim sum cards used in old Cantonese teahouses, turning it into a daily mood record card. Stamp one mark each day to note the date and your mood. When you look back, you can revisit your feelings and memories from each day. As traditional dim sum cards are gradually replaced by QR code ordering, this calendar pays tribute to a fading cultural icon and reinterprets the daily life of Cantonese people through design.
